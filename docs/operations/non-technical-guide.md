# How to Use LeeWay Edge RTC — Non-Technical Guide

Welcome! This guide explains, in simple terms, how to interact with the LeeWay Edge RTC service. No coding or technical background is required.

---

## What Is LeeWay Edge RTC?

LeeWay Edge RTC is a system that lets you talk to Agent Lee (an AI assistant) in real time, with live voice and emotions. It connects your voice to Agent Lee and lets you hear responses instantly, just like a phone or video call — but with an AI.

---

## How Do I Use It?

### 1. Open the Web Demo
- Go to the website or link provided by your team (for example: http://localhost:8080 or your company’s special link).
- You’ll see a page with a microphone button and Agent Lee’s avatar or name.

### 2. Start a Conversation
- Click the microphone button to start talking.
- Speak your question or message out loud.
- Agent Lee will listen, think, and then reply to you with a real voice.

### 3. See and Hear Responses
- You’ll hear Agent Lee’s answer through your speakers or headphones.
- You might also see text or emotions on the screen as Agent Lee responds.

---

## What Can I Ask or Say?
- You can ask Agent Lee anything you’d ask a smart assistant: questions, requests, or even just chat.
- Example: “What’s the weather today?” or “Tell me a joke.”

---

## How Does It Work (In Simple Terms)?
- When you talk, your voice is sent securely to the LeeWay system.
- Agent Lee listens, understands, and creates a reply.
- The reply is sent back to you as a real voice, so it feels like a natural conversation.

---

## Do I Need to Install Anything?
- No! Just use the web link in your browser (like Chrome or Edge).
- Make sure your microphone and speakers are working.

---

## Who Can Help Me?
- If you have trouble, contact your team’s technical support or the person who gave you the link.
- For more info, see the main documentation or ask for a demo.

---

**Enjoy talking to Agent Lee!**
